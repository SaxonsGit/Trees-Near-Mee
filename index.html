FAQ's

Common questions about tree services, landscaping, and bush removal.

How do I know if a tree needs to be removed?
Signs include dead limbs, visible rot, leaning, storm damage, root heaving, and branches threatening structures. We’ll confirm during the free inspection.
Can you remove a tree close to a house or power lines?
Many can be removed safely using controlled cuts and sectional removal. If a utility company must be involved, we’ll tell you during inspection.
What’s the best time of year for bush trimming?
It depends on the plant type. Generally, trimming at the right time supports healthy regrowth and reduces disease risk. We’ll recommend the best timing.
Do you haul away debris and leave the yard clean?
Yes—cleanup is part of professional service. We’ll confirm what’s included in your quote so expectations are crystal clear.
What’s the difference between stump grinding and full stump removal?
Grinding removes the stump below grade and is typically faster and less disruptive. Full removal can involve more excavation. We’ll advise based on your goals.
Will stump grinding leave a hole or mess?
Grinding creates wood chips and a shallow depression. We can discuss backfill options and how you want the area finished.
Do you offer financing?
Yes—TSNM offers premium financing options (when eligible) to spread out costs and keep the project affordable.
How does the 12-month fixed quote help me?
It protects you from price swings and inflation. You know exactly what the work will cost and can plan confidently.
How quickly can you schedule an inspection?
Scheduling depends on demand, but we aim to respond quickly and get you on the calendar as soon as possible.
Are your inspections really free and no-obligation?
Yes. We inspect, provide a clear quote, and you decide—no pressure.
